Emergency Response Guide

Select an emergency scenario to get advice:

Earthquake:
1. Drop, Cover, and Hold On.
2. Stay indoors if you're inside.
3. Move away from windows and heavy objects.
Flood:
1. Move to higher ground immediately.
2. Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
3. Disconnect electrical appliances.
Bomb Threat:
1. Evacuate the area immediately if instructed.
2. Do not touch suspicious items.
3. Call emergency services.
Missile Strike:
1. Move to a basement or reinforced room.
2. Stay away from windows and doors.
3. Cover yourself with heavy materials for protection.
Tsunami:
1. Move inland or to higher ground immediately.
2. Avoid the coast until the all-clear is given.
3. Listen to official warnings and alerts.
